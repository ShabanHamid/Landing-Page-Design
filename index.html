Proof-of-Meme

$SHOCK tokens are paid out to community members when
they generate #SHOCK’D memes that get featured on our
Twitter page.

The process:

1

Submit your #SHOCK’D memes to our meme
repository (coming soon).

2

The best memes are selected and posted to our
Twitter page.

3

The meme creator receives an initial amount of
$SHOCK tokens for their contribution.

4

Additional rewards are paid out based on the
levels of engagement their post receives over the
next 30 days!
